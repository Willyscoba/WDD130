White Water Adventure

Experience the thrill of navigating through rapids on our White Water Adventure trip. This exhilarating journey takes you down the roaring river, surrounded by breathtaking scenery. With experienced guides leading the way, you'll tackle challenging rapids and enjoy moments of calm as you float through serene stretches of water.

Duration: 2 days

Location: River Rapids, Northern Wilderness

Difficulty: Intermediate

River Expedition

Embark on an unforgettable River Expedition with us. This multi-day journey will take you deep into the heart of nature, where you'll navigate through remote stretches of river, camp under the stars, and explore hidden gems along the way. Our expert guides will ensure your safety while providing insights into the surrounding flora and fauna.

Duration: 4 days

Location: Remote River Valley

Difficulty: Advanced

Family Float Trip

Looking for a fun and family-friendly adventure? Our Family Float Trip is perfect for all ages. Drift along gentle currents, soak in the sunshine, and enjoy quality time with your loved ones amidst stunning natural scenery. Our guides will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, with opportunities for swimming and riverside picnics.

Duration: 1 day

Location: Scenic River Valley

Difficulty: Easy
